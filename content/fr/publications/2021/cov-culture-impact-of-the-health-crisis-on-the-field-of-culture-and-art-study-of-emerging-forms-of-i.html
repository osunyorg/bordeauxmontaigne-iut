Study of emerging forms of innovation full_width: true design: full_width: true toc: present: false offcanvas: false hal: docid: "4443443" url: "https://hal.science/hal-04443443v1" abstract: >- Our research project aims to study the impact of the Covid crisis on the professional cultural sector in the Nouvelle-Aquitaine region. To achieve this objective, the research team has joined forces with several stakeholders in a participatory process. Initial consultations with artists from various disciplines show that the crisis has affected the relationship to time and space in terms of the creative process and the relationship to audiences and inhabitants. It also reveals the need to rethink the organisation of the cultural system in order to better support artists and include them in the decision-making process. citation_full: >- Sarah Montero, Aurélie Chêne.

In this regard, Cyber-Physical and Human Systems open a new window on intelligent systems in which the role of humans is prominent. Modeling and simulation (M&S) are recognized as practical tools playing a role in promoting the design, analysis, and development of CPHS. However, from a conceptual and technical point of view, CPHS modeling is challenging for modelers since they face some concepts and processes regarding human beings and intelligent artifacts which were not pervasive in M&S before. Thus, the aim of this study is twofold. The first is to shine new lights on the CPHS understanding regardless of application domains by providing an ontological model.
